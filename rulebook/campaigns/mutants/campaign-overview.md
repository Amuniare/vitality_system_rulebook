# Mutants Campaign Overview

## Setting
**Seattle, 2030** - A world where mutants make up approximately 5% of the population, living in an uneasy balance with baseline humans.

## Core Themes
- **Appearance:** The visible divide between mutants who can "pass" as human and those with obvious physical mutations
- **Double Life:** The strain of maintaining civilian identities while operating as vigilantes
- **Mutant Excellence:** The impossible standard of being "one of the good ones" that society demands
- **The Trolley Problem:** Difficult moral choices with no perfect solutions
- **Systems of Inequality:** How institutional power structures perpetuate discrimination and control

## Current Campaign Status
The campaign has reached a critical climax with the emergency mayoral election following the gala attack. Varia's transformation into a rogue Override-style threat has created an existential crisis that will test all alliances and beliefs.

## Major Ongoing Plots
- **Emergency Election:** Multiple candidates vying for control of Seattle's future
- **Varia's Transformation:** Former teammate now controlling Sentinel robot army
- **Factional Warfare:** ULMH, MLF, and criminal organizations in open conflict
- **Refugee Crisis:** Displaced mutants overwhelming city resources
- **Family Revelations:** Ancient bloodlines and immortal connections affecting current events
