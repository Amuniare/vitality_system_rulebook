# Player Characters

## Character Backgrounds

### Henry (Marquis)
- **Powers:** Bone manipulation and control
- **Background:** Former EMT turned vigilante after discovering corruption in medical system
- **Family:** Son of immortal Bri, half-brother to Hellbeast and related to Achilles
- **Relationships:** Dating Cecilia Monroe, close with team despite ideological differences
- **Arc:** Evolution from reluctant participant to committed MLF member

### Lucille (Duchess)
- **Powers:** Marble manipulation and sensing
- **Background:** Former corporate employee radicalized by sister's betrayal
- **Family:** Sister Vivienne is part of Project Element composite hero
- **Relationships:** Strong bonds with team, leadership tendencies
- **Arc:** From seeking family redemption to embracing revolutionary violence

### Bri (The Ancient)
- **Powers:** Immortality, enhanced durability, ancient knowledge
- **Background:** 3000+ year old immortal with complex family history
- **Family:** Mother to Henry, connected to Hellbeast, Achilles, and other immortals
- **Relationships:** Maternal protective instincts toward team
- **Arc:** Balancing ancient wisdom with modern moral challenges

### Mason (Medic)
- **Powers:** Blood manipulation and hexing abilities
- **Background:** EMT dedicated to preserving life, struggles with team's violent methods
- **Family:** Sister Darlene works at Freaks club with blood powers
- **Relationships:** Moral center of team, often in conflict with violent solutions
- **Arc:** Wrestling with using lethal abilities for greater good

### Neo-Sentinel (Varia)
- **Powers:** Technology interface, Override echoes, growing energy abilities
- **Background:** Khan Corp origins, mysterious connection to Override technology
- **Family:** Complex relationship with Baatar Khan as father figure
- **Relationships:** Bridge between establishment and resistance until breakdown
- **Arc:** From hopeful reformer to corrupted threat requiring team to stop her

### The Vagrant (William)
- **Powers:** Fear manipulation, supernatural stealth, tunnel network knowledge
- **Background:** Off-grid lifestyle, guardian to Salt and Pepper twins
- **Family:** Wards Salt and Pepper, lost Pepper in gala attack
- **Relationships:** Protective of chosen family, suspicious of authorities
- **Arc:** From loner to team member to grief-driven avenger

### Florian [Deceased]
- **Powers:** Sound manipulation and media influence
- **Background:** Journalist turned activist, killed during election chaos
- **Family:** Uncle Hunter Gravy in hiding as former MLF member
- **Relationships:** Struggled between media career and team loyalty
- **Arc:** Corruption from neutral observer to partisan killer to martyr

## Team Dynamics
The Rose Guard formed as masked vigilantes but evolved into something more complex as personal relationships deepened and ideological differences emerged. The team's greatest strength - their diversity of backgrounds and approaches - also became their greatest weakness when external pressures forced them to choose between competing values and loyalties.

## Character Connections
- **Henry & Bri:** Mother-son relationship complicated by immortal family dynamics
- **Mason & Henry:** Former EMT colleagues with shared medical background
- **Lucille & Vivienne:** Sisters divided by opposing philosophies about mutant integration
- **William & Twins:** Chosen family devastated by Pepper's death
- **Varia & Khan:** Corporate father-daughter relationship transcending business interests
