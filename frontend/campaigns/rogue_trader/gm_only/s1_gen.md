# Session 1: The Silent Scream of Hesperus Relay

## Session Goals
- **Warhammer Grimdark Feel**
- **Power Fantasy** - Make players feel like gods (Space Marine crushing regular humans, minor threat from tank)
- **Plot Introduction** - Natural exposition of setting and story arc

---

## Opening: The Hook

**Setting**: Eternal Vigil navigating Spectre Nebula, charting safe paths for the Imperium. Slow progress, short jumps, salvaging from destroyed Explorator vessels.

**The Scream**: Senior Astropath gasps and collapses:
- "Hesperus Prime Relay... under attack! They came... from the green moons... Veridia... not pirates... their eyes... wrong! Choral scream... the Governor is deaf... they're trying to breach the old archives... Seraph's Echo... warning... it sleeps... must not..."

**Result**: Energy surge detected, then silence. Spectre Nebula flares, cutting all communication.

---

## Location 1: Hesperus Prime Relay

**Setting**: Mangled space station wreck, sections vented, no lights, impact scars. Main hub for 3 planets.

**Enemies**: ~100 farmer rebels still aboard, gathering last weapons/supplies

**Key Discoveries**:
- **Bodies**: Crew killed by melee + accurate las-fire. Attackers in agri-world clothing
- **Xenos Hints**: Unnaturally tough chitin, bony spurs, luminous eyes, alien psychic residue
- **Attack Pattern**: Fought toward Astropathic Sanctum and "ARCHIVES – MAXIMA SECRETUM"
- **Astropathic Sanctum**: Utter carnage, evidence of corruption/psychic manipulation attempt
- **Breached Vault**: Plasma-cut door, data destroyed except one shielded console

**Intel Available** (Tech-Use rolls):
- **Seraph Echo**: Ancient Navy survey, "Anomalous Energy Signature" on Thanatos moon, investigation "indefinitely postponed"
- **Thanatos Data**: Clan structure (Bonehewers, Ash Walkers, Sky Serpents), "Keepers of the Old Paths" mentioned
- **Trade Records**: Veridia moons produce 300% system needs - surplus destinations unknown
- **Staff Transfers**: Several requested off-station citing "concerning noble house activities"

---

## Location 2: Hesperus Prime

**Setting**: Massive civilized planet with 3 green agri-moons. Thanatos death world visible in distance.

**Governor Hax**: "Rogue Trader? Old wives tale..." *sees Space Marine* *gulps*
- Paranoid, corrupt, terrified of Inquisition
- Blames "ungrateful agri-workers," no xenos mention
- Secret vaults with plundered archeotech

**Political Intel**:
- **House Valerius**: Ancient nobles, study pre-Imperial lore, seek "rightful stewardship"
- **House Glavius**: Hax's enforcers, control Nebula smuggling
- **Cardinal Ignatio**: Obsessed with prophecies, mentions "signs in green moons"

**Chaos Cult Hints**:
- **"Gilded Thorn"** (Slaaneshi): Decadent nobility with "rare imported items"
- **"Silent Syllogism"** (Tzeentchian): Bureaucrats asking about "stellar alignments" and "echo phenomena"

**System Status**: 8 months isolated, Nebula unusually active, authorities making "alternative arrangements"

---

## Location 3: Veridian Moons

**Options**:
- **Veridia Prima**: Normal harvests, limited chatter
- **Veridia Secundus**: Radio silence, should be peak season (PRIMARY TARGET)
- **Veridia Tertius**: Distress calls, "work accidents," "contaminated shipments"

**Veridia Secundus Discoveries**:
- **Genestealer Cult "The Verdant Kin"** in control
- **Leadership**: Patriarch "Father Verdure," Magus Krell, Primus Gor
- **Infrastructure**: Repurposed agri-tech for war, subterranean warrens
- **Goal**: Preparing for "Star Children" arrival, seeking Seraph's Echo
- **Resistance**: Small pockets of uninfected humans hiding

---

## Enemies

**Basic Farmers**: Scythes, knives
**PDF-Armed Rebels**: Lasguns, stolen bolter
**Vehicles**: Custom armored agri-vehicle, harvester, spiked grinders  
**Rocket Teams**: Stolen launchers, requires charge-up and targeting

**Genestealer Markers**: Three arms, weird eye colors, psychic residue, chitin under clothing
