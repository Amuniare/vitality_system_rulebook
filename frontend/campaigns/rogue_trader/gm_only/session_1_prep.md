## Hesperus Prime - Extended Intelligence

**Political Factions** (through social interaction/investigation):
- **House Valerius**: Ancient nobles seeking "rightful stewardship," rumored to study pre-Imperial lore
- **House Glavius**: Hax's thuggish enforcers, control smuggling through Nebula
- **Cardinal Ignatio**: Obsessed with "prophecies" about system's fate, mentions "signs in the green moons"

**Chaos Cult Hints** (Awareness/Inquiry rolls):
- **Slaaneshi "Gilded Thorn"**: Nobility displaying unusual decadence, possess "rare imported items" 
- **Tzeentchian "Silent Syllogism"**: Bureaucrats asking strange questions about "stellar alignments" and "echo phenomena"

**Governor Hax Intel**:
- Terrified of Inquisition due to "irregularities in tithe assessments"
- Secret vaults contain plundered archeotech and "items of questionable providence"
- Recently increased security around government districts, citing "seditious elements"

**System Isolation**:
- Spectre Nebula has been "unusually active" - blocking even short-range astropathic communication
- No Imperial contact for 8 months, longest isolation in centuries
- Local authorities increasingly desperate, making "alternative arrangements" for governance
