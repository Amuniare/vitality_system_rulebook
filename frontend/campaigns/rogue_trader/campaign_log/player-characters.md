# **Cinder - Unsanctioned Pyromancer Psycher**

## **Character Creation Choices**
- **Heir Ambition: Survivor** 
  - Needs Dame Venecia's authority to shield her from Inquisition persecution
- **Bond with Rogue Trader: The Reluctant Ally**
  - She's an unsanctioned psyker hiding from the Imperium
- **Mandate Focus: Military Recruitment**  
  - Focuses on survival, finding ways to stay alive and not go insane by helping others like herself
- **What drove you to leave: Necessity**  
  - The Great Rift's opening and her family's death forced her into exile as a fugitive psyker
- **Authority & Moral Compromises: Ignore authority unless beneficial**  
  - Survival as unsanctioned psyker taught her Imperial authority means death unless she controls the relationship
- **How others see you: Dangerous but useful**  
  - People know she could incinerate them accidentally, but her recruitment skills are invaluable

## **Known Backstory**
- Powers awakened during Great Rift opening (000.M42) when trying to protect family from disease-maddened gangers
- Accidentally killed everyone including her family with uncontrolled purification fire during galactic psychic surge
- Elash Vyre found her during early desperate years and took care of her, taught her survival skills, and how to use the warp
- A few years later, Elash and Cinder found Shas'ur Vash'ya injured and in a wrecked battlesuit, they saved her and she joined up with them
- Joined Dame Venecia's crew 4 years ago (005.M42) seeking redemption and people who might understand her burden

## **Personality** 
- **Quiet and shy**, hides in corners but becomes talkative one-on-one with people she's comfortable with, won't initiate conversations
- **Compulsively obsessed** with cleanliness and purity, terrified her definition of "contamination" might expand to include friends

## **Powers/Abilities**
- **Purification fire** that burns away at both the targets physical body and their soul
- **Moderate Control** - has learned to manage her powers but it takes time and concentration to safely manifest them

## **Relationships with Others**
- **Dame Venecia** - Coddles her like other psykers; Cinder sees her as hope (authority who survived Warp changes)
- **Elash Vyre** - He taught her what she knows, he's an irresponsible parental figure
- **Shas'ur Vash'ya** - Met her just before joining Dame Venecia's crew, they've become close friends over the last 4 years
- **Sister Innes** - Scares Cinder due to her hatred of psykers and love of burning things
- **Faust Gray** - Gets along well with him because he's nice and friendly to her
